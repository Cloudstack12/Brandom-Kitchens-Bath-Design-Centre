10 Bathroom Renovation Tips that Add Value to Your Home



The bathroom is one of the most critical spaces in a home, but it is often overlooked when it comes to renovation. However, if you are considering selling your home or want to increase its value, renovating your bathroom can significantly improve your home’s overall look and feel. With that in mind, we have put together a list of ten bathroom renovation tips that can add value to your home. These tips range from simple upgrades like new fixtures and fresh coats of paint to more extensive renovations like increasing the size of your bathroom or installing a new shower or tub. Whether renovating your bathroom for enjoyment or adding value to your home, our tips will help you make the most of this virtual space.

1. Upgrade Your Fixtures

Upgrading bathroom fixtures, including faucets, showerheads, and towel racks, can add value to your home cost-effectively and efficiently. Outdated fixtures can impact the bathroom’s look and function, and upgrading them can improve efficiency and modernize its appearance.

List of Recommended Fixtures to Upgrade

  • Faucets – Upgrading faucets can enhance bathroom appearance and functionality. Opt for water-efficient options to save money on water bills.
  • Shower Heads – Replacing shower heads can improve water pressure and coverage. Look for models with adjustable spray patterns for a customized shower experience. Consider large rain heads that provide a soothing experience.
  • Towel racks and hooks – Upgrading towel racks and hooks adds elegance and provides more space to hang towels and bathrobes.
  • Grab Bars – When properly supported, provide safety in the shower, tub and toilet areas.
  • Toilet – Replacing old toilets with water-efficient models can save money on water bills and improve bathroom hygiene. Dual-flush technology provides even more water savings. Consider bidet toilets that save on buying toilet paper.
  • Lighting – Updating bathroom lighting can add brightness and depth. Choose energy-efficient models with adjustable light settings for a customizable experience.

2. Add More Storage

Adding storage to your bathroom can keep the space tidy and functional, making it more enjoyable to use and increasing its overall value.

List of Storage Ideas

  • Vanity with Drawers and Cabinets – Adding a vanity with ample drawers and cabinets helps keep bathroom essentials organized and out of sight.
  • Over-the-Toilet Shelves provide additional storage space for towels, toilet paper, and other essentials.
  • Wall-Mounted Shelves – offers storage for small items like soap, shampoo, and other toiletries.
  • Medicine Cabinets – keep medications and personal items out of sight while still keeping them accessible.
  • Built-in Shelving – adds storage and a unique design element to the bathroom if space is available.

3. Increase the Size of Your Bathroom

Increasing the size of a bathroom can make it more spacious, functional, and comfortable to use and add value to a home by making it more attractive to potential buyers.

List of Tips to Increase the Size of Your Bathroom

  • Knock Down Walls – if possible, consider removing walls to open up your bathroom and create a more open and airy space.
  • Reconfigure your Layout – if knocking down walls is not an option, consider reconfiguring your bathroom layout to better use the existing space.
  • Extend into an Adjacent Room – if you have a neighboring room that needs to be fully utilized, consider extending your bathroom into that space.
  • Add a Skylight – A skylight can bring in more natural light and create a more spacious feel.
  • Choose the Right Color Scheme – using light colors and incorporating reflective surfaces can make your bathroom more extensive and open.

4. Consider Adding a Double Sink

A double sink in your bathroom can provide added convenience and functionality, especially if you share the bathroom with a partner or have a more prominent family. It can also increase the value of your home by making it more appealing to potential buyers.

List of Pros and Cons of a Double Sink


Pros:

  • Convenience – a double sink allows two people to use the bathroom simultaneously, which can be especially helpful for families with children or busy mornings.
  • Increased Storage – a double sink typically comes with extra storage space, which can help store toiletries, towels, and other bathroom essentials.
  • Improved Hygiene – with a double sink, each person can have their own designated space, which can help reduce the spread of germs and bacteria.

Cons:

  • Cost – adding a double sink can be more expensive than a single sink, especially if you need to reconfigure your bathroom layout or plumbing.
  • A double sink can take up more space, which is only feasible if your bathroom is ample.
  • Maintenance – cleaning two sinks and faucets can be more time-consuming and require more upkeep than a single sink.

5. Install Energy-Efficient Lighting

Installing energy-efficient lighting in your bathroom can help you save on your energy bill and reduce your carbon footprint. Energy-efficient lighting consumes less energy and lasts longer than traditional lighting, making it cost-effective and environmentally friendly. Furthermore, the proper lighting can improve your bathroom’s overall look and feel, making it more inviting and comfortable.

List of Recommended Energy-Efficient Lighting Options

  • LED lighting – is energy-efficient, long-lasting, and customizable with various color temperatures and brightness levels.
  • CFL lighting – is another energy-efficient option that provides bright, warm light and is available in different color temperatures and brightness levels.
  • Motion-Activated Lighting – lighting can help save energy by turning off lights when the bathroom is not in use, making it ideal for families with children.
  • Dimmer Switches – Dimmer switches can help save energy and create a relaxing atmosphere by allowing you to adjust the brightness of your lights.

6. Incorporate a New Shower or Tub

Upgrading your shower or tub can improve your bathroom’s functionality, luxury, and value. A new shower or tub can create a more relaxing experience and add convenience, making it more enjoyable.

List of Recommended Showers and Tubs

  • Walk-in Shower – is a modern and spacious option that can be customized to fit any size and style of the bathroom, adding a contemporary look.
  • Soaking Tub – adds a spa-like experience to the bathroom, comes in various sizes and shapes, and can be installed in any bathroom for a touch of luxury and increased home value.
  • Clawfoot Tub – adds a vintage and traditional look to the bathroom. It comes in different colors, styles, and materials and is an excellent option for those who prefer a classic aesthetic.
  • Bathtub-Shower Combo – this is a versatile option that provides both a bathtub and a shower and can be designed to fit any bathroom size, making it ideal for smaller spaces.

7. Add a Fresh Coat of Paint

Adding a fresh coat of paint to a bathroom is a simple way to give it a new and polished look. This can make the space feel cleaner, brighter, and more inviting while covering up any signs of wear and tear on the walls or ceiling.

List of Recommended Paint Colors

  • Soft Gray – is popular for bathrooms because it creates a soothing and relaxing atmosphere. It can also make your bathroom feel more spacious and airy.
  • Light Blue – light blue is another excellent color option for bathrooms. It can create a calming and refreshing atmosphere, making it the perfect choice for a relaxing bath or shower.
  • Beige – is a classic and versatile color that can work well with any bathroom style. It creates a warm and inviting atmosphere that can make your bathroom feel more comfortable and welcoming.
  • Light Green – is a color that can add a natural and calming element to your bathroom. It can make your bathroom feel more peaceful and serene.

8. Upgrade Your Flooring

Upgrading your bathroom flooring can improve the look and functionality of your bathroom, as it is exposed to moisture and wear and tear. Upgrading the bathroom floor can make it look more durable and easier to maintain.

List of Recommended Flooring Options

  • Ceramic or Porcelain Tiles: Waterproof, durable, easy to clean, and available in various sizes, styles, and colors.
  • Vinyl Flooring: Affordable, versatile, can mimic the look of other materials, waterproof, easy to clean, and warm to the touch.
  • Natural Stone: Adds luxury and sophistication and is durable and easy to clean but may require more maintenance.
  • Cork Flooring: Eco-friendly, durable, comfortable to walk on, moisture-resistant, and available in various colors and patterns.

9. Hire a Professional

Hiring a professional for bathroom renovation is essential for safety, efficiency, and avoiding costly mistakes. Professionals have the necessary tools, knowledge, and experience to handle any challenges during the renovation process and ensure your bathroom meets all safety and building codes.

List of Recommended Questions to Ask a Professional Before Hiring

  • Are you licensed and insured?
  • How long have you been in business, and can you provide references or examples of previous work?
  • What is your process for completing a bathroom renovation project, and what is the estimated timeline?
  • What is your approach to project management, and how will you communicate with me throughout the renovation process?
  • How do you handle unexpected issues or changes during the renovation process, and what is your method for resolving them?
  • What is the estimated project cost, and what is included in that cost?
  • Do you offer any warranties or guarantees for the work completed?

10. Add Greenery

Adding plants to your bathroom can enhance the aesthetics and create a soothing atmosphere while improving the air quality by reducing pollutants and moisture.

List of Recommended Plants and Flowers for Your Bathroom

  • Spider Plants – are low-maintenance and thrive in low-light conditions. They purify the air and add a natural touch to the bathroom.

  • Aloe Vera – is a natural air purifier with medicinal properties that can treat burns and cuts.
  • Peace Lily – Peace Lilies are beautiful plants that thrive in low-light conditions. They can help to remove pollutants from the air and add a touch of tranquility to your bathroom.
  • Orchids – Orchids add elegance and style to the bathroom and require bright, indirect light and a humid environment.

In conclusion, adding value to your home through bathroom renovations can be an excellent investment. You can create a functional and luxurious bathroom that will attract potential buyers by upgrading your fixtures, increasing storage space, installing energy-efficient lighting, adding a fresh coat of paint, and other renovation ideas.
